Strategy Consultant

The term Strategy Consultant is used to describe consultants who operate at the highest level of the consultancy market, with a focus on strategic topics like corporate and organisational strategy, economic policy, government policy and functional strategy. For this reason, strategy consultants generally carry out work assigned by top managers such as CxOs, directors and senior managers. Given that the nature of strategy consulting differs from the other more implementation and operation driven areas, strategy consultants generally have a different profile from their peers. Their focus lies more on quantitative/analytics skills, and their job description revolves more around giving advice than overseeing implementation.
